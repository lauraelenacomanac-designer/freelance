FAQ

Questions you're
Probably wondering.

How quickly can you start?
Typically within 1–2 weeks of the discovery call, depending on my current capacity. I keep my client list intentionally small so every project gets focused attention. Book a call to check current availability.
Do you also do development / Framer builds?
Yes — I have a frontend background and can build in Framer for marketing sites and prototypes. For production web apps I deliver clean Figma specs optimised for your dev stack (Angular, React, etc.).
What if I only need a UX audit, not a full redesign?
Absolutely. A UX audit is actually a great starting point — it gives you a prioritised roadmap of what to fix and why before spending on design or development. It's available as a standalone deliverable outside the packages above.
How do you handle feedback and revisions?
I use structured feedback rounds (clearly defined in the proposal) with Figma comments for async review. This keeps things clear and avoids endless revision cycles. Most projects stay on schedule because the process is tight from the start.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Yes — some of my best work has been for early-stage founders who need to validate fast. I can help you go from concept to a testable prototype in under two weeks, which is often what you need for user testing or investor conversations.
What industries do you specialise in?
B2B SaaS, fintech/banking, mobile apps, gaming, VR/AR, and e-commerce. I've also designed kiosk hardware UX, tvOS experiences, and civic tech apps. If it involves a screen and users, I can help.
